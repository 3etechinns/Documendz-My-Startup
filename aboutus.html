About Us

We're a small team of young passionate people

Documendz.com was founded in 2014 and is based out of Mumbai, India. We are a young, vibrant team, consisting of engineers, designers and people with prior startup experience, in one of the most promising new startups of the country. It excites and motivates us a great deal to work for something that betters people's lives, and doing it with a bunch of highly talented and zealous individuals keeps us driven and asking for more.
