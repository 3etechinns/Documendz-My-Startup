FAQs
What is Documendz?
Documendz is a web service that simplifies the process of getting your documents reviewed by your peers. Whether you wish to get reports reviewed by your boss or your CV reviewed by your seniors or even grade assignments of your students, documendz.com lets you do all this without going through the pain of mailing documents or sharing them over drives. Upload once and share with multiple reviewers to get a thorough assessment of your work and leverage the power of unhindered collaboration over cloud.
How can I use Documendz?
You can start using Documendz by signing up on www.documendz.com to get your free profile. You are entitled to a basic(free) plan on sign up. Once you are there, you can create workgroups, upload files in it, add members to workgroups and start collaborating in realtime.
What does the Basic(free) plan include?
In the basic plan, you can upload a maximum of 50 files and add upto 3 collaborators in a workgroup.
What if I have reached any of the above(basic plan) limits?
You will be given an option to notify us once you reach the maximum limit.
You can also reach out to us over feedback@documendz.com as well!
Is there a sample page on which I can try out the features?
You can try out the sample here even if you haven't signed up.
We also provide a few sample files to try out the service once you create your profile.
What file formats does Documendz support?
We currently support PDF, .doc, .docx and all popular image types.
What is the maximum allowed individual file size?
You can upload a file with a maximum size of upto 4MB.
However, you can always let us know if you would like to increase this limit.
Do I need to save all my annotations everytime?
There is absolutely no need for that. All your changes are automatically saved in realtime.
Do I need to save all my annotations everytime?
To undo/delete an annotation:
  1. Select the annotation from the panel
  2. Right click on the annotation that you would like to delete
  3. This will give you an option to delete it
How will I know when someone adds me as a collaborator?
You will be notified via an email
What rights does the workgroup owner & collaborator have?
Workgroup owner: Can add/remove collaborators, upload/delete every file, markup files,delete the workgroup
Collaborator: Can upload a file & markup files.
How can I contact Documendz for support?
We really love to hear from our users. You could simply click on the feedback button on top of the home page and send in your ideas, suggestions or feedback right away ! Alternatively, you can directly write to us at feedback@documendz.com
Which browsers is Documendz compatible with?
Documendz works great on Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari , Opera and IE10+.
How secure are my files on Documendz?
We understand how important your documents are for you. We use premium AWS(Amazon Web services) servers for all our storage. Apart from this all your information transmitted on the our server is encrypted via SSL.
