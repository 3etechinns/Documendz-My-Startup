Documendz Web App

View Gmail Attachments and Drive documents directly in Documendz

You can now edit, review and annotate your documents straight out of your inbox – no more wasting time or space downloading that piece of work you want to get done ASAP.
It is this simple: next time you open your docs in Gmail, you have the option of conveniently annotating your files through Documendz, be it of any file type: doc, docx, pdf, ppt, pptx and all popular file formats.

How it works in Gmail?

  1. Open the attachment in your mail
  2. Click on Open with at the header
  3. From the drop down menu, go to the Connect more apps option – you will find Documendz in the list.
  4. Once you’ve chosen it, you can Connect it to your Gmail.

How it works in Google Drive?

  1. Right click on the file you want to open
  2. Click on Open with
  3. From the drop down menu, go to the Connect more apps option – you will find Documendz in the list.
  4. Once you’ve chosen it, you can Connect it to your Gmail.

The next time you want to open an attachment from Gmail or Google Drive, you will automatically find Documendz in the Open with option and you will not ever have to go looking again.This way, you save considerable time, effort and even disk space, and smoothly get your work done.

Have a happy time editing with Documendz all new Web App.
