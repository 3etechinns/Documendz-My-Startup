About us


We are a young, vibrant team, consisting of engineers, designers and people with prior startup experience, in one of the most promising new startups of the country, so working here is damn exciting, to say the least! We believe in equality rather than hierarchy, originality over routine, performance rather than power and passion over obligation. It excites and motivates us a great deal to work for something that betters people's lives, and doing it with a bunch of highly talented and zealous individuals keeps us driven and asking for more. In a nutshell, at Documendz, you will be encouraged and inspired to be yourself, and to learn, grow, challenge and become your best. The results will be amazing and infinite!

If you have the thirst to prove yourself, love challenges, are creative and innovative, Documendz is the place for you. As an integral part of a young startup, you will be involved in very crucial roles of decision-making and will be instrumental in shaping up the company. We reward zealous, passionate and highly driven people and expect them to be good team players.
