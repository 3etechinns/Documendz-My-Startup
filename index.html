Documendz.com was founded in 2014 and is based out of Mumbai, India. It is a web service that simplifies the process of getting your documents reviewed by your peers.

Whether you wish to get reports reviewed by your boss or your CV reviewed by your seniors or even grade assignments of your students, documendz.com lets you do all this without going through the pain of mailing documents or sharing them over drives. Upload once and share with multiple reviewers to get a thorough assessment of your work and leverage the power of unhindered collaboration over cloud.
