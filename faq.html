What is Documendz?
Documendz is a web service that simplifies the process of getting your documents reviewed by your peers. Whether you wish to get reports reviewed by your boss or your CV reviewed by your seniors or even grade assignments of your students, documendz.com lets you do all this without going through the pain of mailing documents or sharing them over drives. Upload once and share with multiple reviewers to get a thorough assessment of your work and leverage the power of unhindered collaboration over cloud.
What is the maximum number of files I can upload?
Currently, the maximum number of files that can be uploaded is 30
How is the file upload limit calculated?
Files once uploaded will be counted in the maximum limit
What is the maximum individual file size that is allowed?
File size allowed is upto 4Mb
What file formats does Documendz support?
Currently pdf and all kinds of image files(png,jpg,bmp,svg,swf) are supported
How will I know when someone asks me for reviewing his/her file?
You will be intimidated by an email
What happens when an author deletes a particular file?
In such a case, the original file along with all the copies gets deleted from the author’s account. Not only this, the file also gets deleted from the reviewer ’s account(with whom the original file was shared)
What if the file upload limit is reached?
In such a scenario please contact us for further details
How can I get in touch with you?
We really love to hear from our users. Simply click on the feedback button on top of the home page and you can send in your ideas, suggestions or feedback right away !
Which browsers is Documendz compatible with?
Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Opera
How can I keep a track of all the documents that I have uploaded?
Simply create an account with us for free and you will be able to manage all your documents in a single place
